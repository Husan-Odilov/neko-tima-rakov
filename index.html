About NekoInn



NekoInn is more than a cat boarding facility; it's a home
away from home for your furry family members. Nestled
in serene surroundings, our inn offers a tranquil retreat for
cats of all shapes, sizes, and personalities. With a team of
dedicated caregivers passionate about feline welfare, we
ensure every guest receives love, attention, and comfort.
Rest easy knowing your cat is in caring hands at NekoInn.
